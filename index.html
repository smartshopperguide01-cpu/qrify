Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common queries regarding QR patterns, scanning limits, and privacy protocols.

Are there limitations on scanning limits with these codes?

No. The QR codes generated by QRify are static matrix patterns that contain encoded information natively. Because there is no intermediary routing server, there are no limits, expiration cycles, or licensing costs.

How does the local client model protect data privacy?

Unlike traditional SaaS utilities that transmit data arrays back to cloud servers, QRify renders and parses your inputs directly in your browser. Sensitive credentials, including Wi-Fi security keys and business cards, never transit network channels during generation.

What file format should be exported for high-quality printing?

For physical prints, corporate signage, or restaurant menus, select the SVG vector format or output images with dimensions above 2000px. Vector graphics scale infinitely without resolution loss.

How should logo size and spacing parameters be chosen?

Keep embedded brand logos under 25% scale of the overall matrix area. Setting Error Correction Level (ECC) parameters to Q or H ensures adequate safety padding for scanners to decode obscured data zones reliably.
